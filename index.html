Welcome

Victoria Park is one of Cardiff’s best-loved traditional parks. Opened in 1897 , the park is situated in the western suburb of Canton. Covering 20 acres, there are many mature trees and colourful flower borders which blossom in spring and wide range of facilities continue to have year-round appeal for all ages.

There are wide accessible paths. Public music events, markets and fairs take place throughout the summer months. Historical features within the park have been preserved and continue to bring joy to park visitors today.

Victoria Park is one of the city’s Green Flag parks.

Victoria Park is open daily from 07:30 until 30 minutes before sunset all year round.

Things to do

Victoria Park has a vast play area and in the summer months you can also visit the newly refurbished splash pad with water features spread across three zones for toddlers and older children.

In addition to play areas, Victoria Park offers an array of amenities, which include:

  • Beach volley ball club
  • Multi use games area: open all year round – no charges
  • Refreshment kiosk Open throughout the year
  • Tennis courts: open all year round – no charges
  • Public toilets and baby changing facilities: located near to children’s playground
